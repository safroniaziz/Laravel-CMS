- PHP: 8.2+
- Database: SQLite/MySQL/PostgreSQL
- ORM: Eloquent

**Frontend**
- CSS: Bootstrap 5
- JS: jQuery 3.7
- Icons: Font Awesome 6.4
- Design: Responsive, Mobile-first

**Architecture**
- Pattern: MVC
